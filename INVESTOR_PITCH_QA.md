## Body Language & Delivery Tips

### Do's
✅ Maintain eye contact
✅ Speak confidently (you know your business)
✅ Use data (numbers build credibility)
✅ Tell stories (make it memorable)
✅ Show passion (investors invest in people)
✅ Listen actively (don't interrupt)
✅ Admit what you don't know (honesty builds trust)

### Don'ts
❌ Don't oversell (be realistic)
❌ Don't badmouth competitors (focus on your strengths)
❌ Don't get defensive (questions are opportunities)
❌ Don't use jargon (explain simply)
❌ Don't rush (pause, think, answer)

## Red Flags to Avoid

❌ "We have no competition" (shows lack of research)
❌ "We'll figure it out later" (shows lack of planning)
❌ "Everyone is our customer" (shows lack of focus)
❌ "We just need marketing" (shows product-market fit issues)
❌ "Trust me, it'll work" (shows lack of data)

✅ Instead: Be specific, data-driven, realistic, prepared
