# Tradigoo Seller Guidelines

## 1. Overview

Tradigoo requires all wholesalers to follow standardized guidelines to ensure transparency, quality control, and trust in wholesale transactions. These guidelines help reduce disputes and improve successful order completion rates.

---

## 2. Seller Verification

All wholesalers must:

- Complete identity verification.
- Provide valid business registration details.
- Submit required compliance documentation.
- Maintain accurate business information on the platform.

Verification helps build trust with retailers.

---

## 3. Product Listing Standards

When listing products, sellers must:

- Provide accurate product descriptions.
- Clearly mention specifications (size, material, model, etc.).
- Upload clear and genuine product images.
- Declare minimum order quantity (MOQ).
- Mention estimated dispatch timelines.

Misleading listings are not permitted.

---

## 4. Pricing Transparency

Sellers must:

- Provide transparent bulk pricing.
- Clearly mention any additional charges (shipping, packaging, taxes).
- Avoid hidden fees after order confirmation.

All pricing must be clearly visible before order placement.

---

## 5. Order Fulfillment Responsibilities

After receiving an order:

- Prepare products according to invoice.
- Ensure correct quantity and quality.
- Securely package items to prevent damage.
- Upload shipment details before dispatch.

Failure to dispatch within the committed timeline may affect seller ratings.

---

## 6. Packaging Standards

Sellers must:

- Use secure and damage-resistant packaging.
- Seal packages properly.
- Ensure labels are accurate and readable.
- Avoid mixing incorrect products in bulk shipments.

Proper packaging reduces delivery disputes.

---

## 7. Cooperation During Disputes

If a dispute is raised:

- Sellers must respond within the defined time frame.
- Provide supporting documents such as invoice copy and shipment proof.
- Cooperate with AI and manual verification processes.

Non-cooperation may result in delayed payment release.

---

## 8. Performance and Ratings

Seller performance may be evaluated based on:

- On-time dispatch rate
- Dispute frequency
- Resolution quality
- Retailer feedback

Consistent policy violations may impact platform visibility.

---

## 9. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are not allowed:

- Supplying counterfeit goods
- Providing false product information
- Manipulating invoices
- Attempting to bypass escrow payments
- Encouraging off-platform transactions

Violation of these rules may lead to account suspension.

---
